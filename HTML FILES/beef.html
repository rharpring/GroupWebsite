Beef

Catalpa Acres has been raising beef catlle for almost one hundred years now. Our herd is made up of many different breed from over the years, primarily black angus, gernsey, Jersey, and herferds. They are raised in a grassy pasture with creek water and are fed hay in teh cold winters. When they are chsoen for slaughtered they are finished on corn feed, so they do not taste gamey. We do not process them ourselves, but we take them to USDA certified processsors.
