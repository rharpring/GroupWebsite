Farm to Family: Catalpa Acres

Nestled at the edge of the bustling Boone County Farmers Market, the Catalpa Acres tent is a warm, inviting stall filled with vibrant, locally grown produce. For almost two decades, we have built a loyal following, offering everything from heirloom tomatoes and fresh greens to homemade jams, freshly baked pies, and a variety of farm-fresh beef and pork. With our smiling faces and welcoming spirit, we pride ourselves on offering not only the best of what our farm has to offer but also a personal touch that makes every customer feel like part of the extended family. Whether it’s a quick chat about the best way to prepare ground beef or a friendly recommendation for a new recipe, our stand has become a cornerstone of the market—where quality, community, and tradition come together with every transaction.

At Catalpa Acres, we specialize in sustainable farming, offering a variety of organic products. Feel free to explore our products, locations, and learn more about us on the pages linked.
