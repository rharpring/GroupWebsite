Our Story

Catalpa Arces got started in the 1930's when Virgina Shadler bought her mother and father's farm,
and decided to make it her own. She married Arlis Kimberlin and they raised dairy and beef cattle.
The farm is now on its fifth generation, including Virginia's Parents.
Pictured to the left is Raymond Kimberlin Virgina and Arlis' son. They also had a daughter Patsy.
The Farm was passed down the Raymond, who then married Beverly Mcnabb.
They then had Scott and Stacy
Scott currently runns the farm. He married Staphanie Cooley. He has three children
Courtney, Sadie, and Carter. Together they continue to raise cattle, poultry, and produce crops.
